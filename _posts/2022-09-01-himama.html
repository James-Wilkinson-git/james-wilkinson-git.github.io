I served as the exclusive front-end engineer at HiMama, where a significant portion of my role involved instructing the full-stack developers on React and JavaScript best practices.

I devised a comprehensive strategy for migrating the outdated jQuery views to React, alongside a technological roadmap outlining the progression from the current state to a future encompassing micro-services, multiple single-page applications, and the potential adoption of a unified or monorepot paradigms.

Additionally, I designed a phased approach, complete with a decision matrix to determine when the implementation of the old technology is more suitable compared to the new technology.
